// SYSTEM LOG

The Journey

2022

ECE Journey Begins

Enrolled in Electronics and Communication Engineering at AAR Mahaveer Engineering College, JNTU Hyderabad. First encounter with circuit theory, electronics fundamentals, and the magic of embedded systems.

2022–23

First Circuits, First Code

Hands-on experiments with Arduino, basic sensors, and simple circuits. Discovered the powerful synergy between hardware and software — and fell in love with making things that actually work in the physical world.

2023

ESP32 & IoT Deep Dive

Upgraded to ESP32 ecosystem. Built first Wi-Fi connected projects, mastered I2C/SPI protocols, integrated MAX30102 and BME280 sensors, and published data to cloud dashboards via MQTT.

2024

VitaWatch — Wearable Leap

Designed and built VitaWatch from scratch using ESP32-S3 Zero — miniaturizing everything into a wearable health monitor. Solved real hardware challenges: power management, sensor accuracy, and form factor constraints.

2025

Vital Nova Pod — Major Project

Conceived and developed Vital Nova Pod as a JNTUH major project — a full IoT infant healthcare system. Explored AI-based health prediction concepts, parent notification systems, and human-centered product design.

2026 →

Graduating & Building Forward

B.Tech ECE graduate. Actively exploring Edge AI, TinyML, and next-generation wearable health tech. Targeting product development roles and building toward founding an embedded tech startup.
